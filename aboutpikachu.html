All About Pikachu

Everyones favorite pokémon

Pikachu is the electric rat pokémon. It is the evolved form of pichu, and the unevolved form of Raichu. Pikachu is without a doubt the most popular pokémon. In the pokemon anime ash recieves pikachu after arriving late to professor Oak's lab. The pikachu in the anime does not like to go in its pokeball and does not wish to evolve into a raichu.
